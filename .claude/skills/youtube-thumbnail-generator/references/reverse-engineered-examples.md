# Reverse-engineered examples (proven thumbnails → prompts)

Breakdowns of high-performing reference thumbnails (images in
`memory/inspiration/reference-thumbnails/`), each with the prompt you'd write to recreate it.
Adapt these for Leo (swap the subject to his face refs + orange brand). Two creators:
**A = photoreal AI-tools / income niche (most relevant to Leo)**, **B = stylized 3D character.**

## ⭐ Element library — the reusable techniques (apply to Leo)
- **Floating glossy app/tool icons** the creator holds or that float around him — instantly shows
  the tools/software used (rounded-square icons with soft shadows + gloss). HUGE for Leo's
  AI/automation/CRM content (show the actual tools).
- **Money / result proof:** flying dollar bills, a "$X/day" number, or a rising revenue chart with
  a specific callout (date + exact $ like "$1,573.89"). Builds credibility + curiosity.
- **Extreme shock pose over a laptop** (mouth wide open, eyes wide, leaning in) — top scroll-stopper
  for "I did X with AI" videos.
- **Floating result cards** around the creator — show outputs (ads, posts, dashboards, client cards)
  as glossy rounded cards/phones with soft shadows.
- **Red marker annotation** — handwritten red title + underline + a red curved arrow connecting two
  things. Guides the eye, feels like a teardown.
- **Topic-illustrated background** — a hand-drawn storyboard/whiteboard of the topic behind the
  creator; OR a blurred "wall of content" (editing timelines, thumbnails, dashboards) showing
  scale/expertise.
- **Styled wardrobe** for a premium angle (e.g. a bold-coloured blazer) — memorable, aspirational.
- **Glowing bold text + dramatic spotlights** (creator B) for high-impact, plus a small YouTube
  play logo as a niche signal.
- Common to all: ONE big bold headline (condensed sans, hard outline/shadow), strong emotion, glossy
  premium finish, clear focal hierarchy.

## A1 — "AI Animation" (storyboard whiteboard)
**Why it works:** the topic is shown *literally* as hand-drawn sketches; the drawn caricature of
himself is clever + memorable; warm "creative studio" editorial feel; red marker title + arrow guide
the eye. Types: creator + topic-illustration + annotation.
**Prompt:**
> Professional YouTube thumbnail, photoreal, warm editorial. A young man [subject] in a navy t-shirt,
> friendly smile, standing centre, pointing to the right. Behind him a large hand-drawn storyboard
> whiteboard covered in pencil sketches — a caricature of himself, a walk-cycle sequence, character
> turnarounds, and handwritten animation notes. Top-left: handwritten red marker title "AI Animation"
> with a red underline and a red curved arrow. Soft warm studio lighting, light background, clean
> creative-studio aesthetic, high detail, premium.

> Small app icon top-left. High energy, glossy, premium.

   hands or around him — his AI/automation videos should *show the stack*.

   type videos.

5. Keep his brand: orange palette, black/charcoal or clean light bg, bold condensed headline with one
   word highlighted, real photographic elements, $10k-designer polish, NO white sticker outline.
