# Example concepts — how Leo likes thumbnails conceived & prompted

These are **Leo's own example concepts**, provided as taste reference — NOT jobs to
generate. Read this before drafting concepts for a real video so the structure, layout
vocabulary, and prompt phrasing match what he actually wants. Mimic this style.

## The pattern Leo uses for a concept
Each concept = a short **Visual:** description (the idea in plain English) + a detailed
**Prompt:** (the literal image prompt). Always include:
- **Composition by thirds.** State left / centre / right explicitly. Leo (orange hoodie)
  almost always sits in the **right third**, with a named expression (confident, gesturing,
  knowing smirk, calm "I know what I'm talking about").
- **One big hook** as the focal text: a number or 2–3 words. Bright **orange `#FF7300`**,
  extra-thick weight (900), tight letter spacing, hard drop shadow, often a thin white
  outline. Secondary text in **white, ALL CAPS**.
- **A supporting visual device behind the text at 25–40% opacity** — a faint icon grid, a
  tilted dashboard mockup, or a mosaic of UI screenshots. Adds "operational proof" texture.
- **A curiosity gap:** blurred/hidden text bars, "+ 9 MORE", "??? → PROFIT", "#1 · 2.4M views".
- **Brand thread:** orange accent + a single orange curved arrow / underline / circle as the
  annotation. The orange **Claude asterisk** appears as a small "powered by" mark on tool videos.

## Two background modes (pick by video type)
- **Mode B — dark (agency / money / "tools" videos):** dark charcoal-to-black gradient,
  premium cinematic vignette. High contrast. Used for the "12 Agency Tools" set.
- **Mode A — light (product/tool demo, e.g. an Instagram tool):** soft off-white with warm
  grey gradient, generous whitespace, charcoal headline in sentence case with a hand-drawn
  underline on the key word (e.g. "Steal viral **hooks**."), real-looking app/post mockups,
  often **no people**. Used for the "Steal Viral Hooks" set.

## Layout devices in his vocabulary (reuse these)
- Massive single numeral ("12") + label beneath.
- Numbered list 1→12 with **blurred white bars** hiding the items (curiosity).
- 3×4 unified **app-icon grid** (iOS-icon aesthetic), most icons muted + 2–3 in orange.
- Icons **fanned/scattered** across the bottom for an "abundance" feel.
- **3 hero icons + "+ 9 MORE"** to dodge the "12 icons turn to mush" legibility problem.
- 2×6 **numbered cards** (glass/frosted tiles).
- **Wall-of-tools mosaic** — dimmed, tilted UI screenshots as full-bleed background texture.
- Two logos + "+" + a real post (series-consistent "X + Instagram" layout).
- Phone mockup + orange **magnifying glass** (research framing).
- **Leaderboard** of ranked posts with a #1 highlight + real view counts.

## Critical workflow note
Leo treats AI output as a **layout base, then composites real assets in Canva**: swap in
real screenshots (Instagram posts, dashboards), real logos (Claude asterisk, Instagram), and
adds orange circles/underlines/arrows digitally. So prompts should aim for a **clean base with
placeholder mockups** and leave room for real assets — don't sweat exact UI text or tiny icons,
since those get replaced. Big hook text + composition + his face + mood are what AI must nail.

## Two representative full examples (match this prompt phrasing)

**Mode B example — "12 Agency Tools" (massive numeral + faint grid):**
> Landscape YouTube thumbnail, dark charcoal-to-black gradient background. Left third
> dominated by a massive bold sans-serif numeral "12" in bright orange (#FF7300), extra-thick
> weight (900), with subtle drop shadow and a thin white outline. Beneath "12", smaller bold
> white text "AGENCY TOOLS" in all caps, tight letter spacing. Centre, behind and around the
> "12": a faint 3x4 grid of stylised app icons at 40% opacity, each in a rounded square (chart,
> video play button, ads dashboard, chat bubble, checklist, CRM card), muted colours. Right
> third: a young man with short dark hair and short beard in a bright orange hoodie, confident
> focused expression, gesturing toward the grid, looking at camera. High contrast, premium,
> slight cinematic vignette.

**Mode A example — "Steal Viral Hooks" (logo + logo + real post):**
> Landscape YouTube thumbnail, soft off-white background with subtle warm grey gradient. Top:
> bold sans-serif text "Steal viral hooks." in deep charcoal, sentence case, with hand-drawn
> black underline beneath "viral". Centred beneath: two large rounded-square app icons with soft
> drop shadows, separated by a bold black "+": left = bright orange (#FF7300) asterisk starburst
> on white; right = Instagram gradient camera icon on white. Beneath the logos: one large vertical
> 9:16 Instagram reel mockup styled like a real screenshot with realistic interface details.
> Premium, clean, generous whitespace, no people.
