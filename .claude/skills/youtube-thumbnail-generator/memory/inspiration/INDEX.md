# Inspiration library

Save thumbnails from other creators here. Capture the *technique* to reuse, not
just the picture. Group related images in subfolders by creator or theme.

Two reference styles were provided. They map cleanly to the two kinds of videos
Fluxomate makes, so treat them as two "modes" to pick from per video.

---

## Style A — Clean Tech / "Claude-Code dev" look
_Best for: AI tooling, Claude Code, product/feature, "X just dropped" videos._

What makes it work:
- **Light, clean backgrounds** (white / soft grey), almost Apple-like. Lots of negative space.
- **A product UI screenshot is the hero** — phone mockup or app screenshot, large and crisp,
  often floating with a soft shadow.
- **Slash-command / lowercase hooks as the title text:** `/free`, `/mobile`, `/routines`.
  Short, monospace-ish, punchy. Also casual lowercase lines: "i almost didn't switch :)",
  "It's too good." Feels like a CLI/dev insider.
- **Hero glowing logo** for big-news videos (the Claude starburst glowing on dark) —
  the product/brand mark IS the focal point.
- **Benchmark bars + big % numbers** for comparisons: "93.9%" vs "80.8%", green-up framing.
- **Competitor logos** (OpenAI, Gemini) lined up for "X dominates" comparison.
- **Creator cut out on the right third**, genuine warm smile, clean even studio light.
- **Orange/red curved hand-drawn arrow** pointing at the key number — note: this is ORANGE,
  ties straight into the Fluxomate brand color.
- **Red X** over a competitor for "this is bad, here's better" framing.
- Text kept to 1–4 words. One idea only.

## Style B — Bold Money / "Agency results" look
_Best for: agency systems, sales, money-model, "I fixed a $Xm business" videos._

What makes it work:
- **Darker, higher-contrast backgrounds.** Heavier, more aggressive than Style A.
- **A specific big dollar figure is the hook:** "$1.4M", "$12.5M", "$6.6M", "$30M".
  Real, concrete numbers beat vague claims.
- **Bold heavy condensed text, often ALL-CAPS:** "FULL BREAKDOWN", "FULL GUIDE".
- **Process / flow framing with arrows + a mystery step:** "MONEY MODEL → YOUTUBE CHANNEL
  → ??? → PROFIT", "AUDIT → CREATIVES → ??? → PROFIT". The "??? → PROFIT" gap drives curiosity.
- **Green-up / red-down line charts** with a big number callout.
- **Expressive creator poses:** pointing, hand-on-chin thinking, reacting. Often a two-person
  "audit / breakdown" whiteboard format for teardown videos.
- **Brand logo + consequence imagery** ("Why your ads stopped working" = Meta logo + burning money).
- **Orange/red curved arrows** pointing at the key metric (again, orange — on brand).
- Whiteboard/diagram screenshots for "system" content (funnels, phases, pipelines).

### More Mode B references — agency/money creators (2nd & 3rd batches)
Higher-budget agency/money channels (Acquisition.com / Hormozi-style + similar). Devices to borrow:
- **Comparison split:** "EVERYONE ELSE / YOU", "NORMAL / CRINGE" — a green-up vs red-down line or
  a red scribble vs clean green arrow, contrasting two outcomes side by side.
- **Numbered whiteboard breakdown:** hand-drawn marker style, "PERFECT BUSINESS" with 1–5 points
  + little doodle icons. Great for listicle/framework videos.
- **Two-person teardown format:** two people at a desk over a whiteboard/laptop ("Fix Your Ads",
  "Do This First", "$2,500,000 Business in 36 Minutes"). Signals a real working session.
- **Red marker annotation on real screenshots:** emails/dashboards with red circles, underlines,
  arrows ("STEAL THESE" with circled email lines, "2X REVENUE" circled). Looks like a live audit.
- **Big dollar figures, often green:** "$1,000,000/Month", "$1M/MO?", "$2,500,000" — green = money.
- **Gauge / meter device:** TRUTH↔LIE detector dial ("Are You Doing $1M/MO?"). Tension + curiosity.
- **Padlock = locked secret:** "3X SHOW RATE" with a padlock over a blurred reveal.
- **Node / flow diagram of a system:** "AGENTIC AI CLONE" with CSM/COACH/SALES/MARKETER nodes —
  perfect for Fluxomate's automation/agent content.
- **Blurred / redacted elements:** dashboards and lists partly blurred to force the click.
- **Yellow or green hook text** as an alternative to white/orange when more punch is needed.
- Expressive poses: pointing at the element, shrugging "how I make money", hands-out presenting.

## Patterns common to BOTH (apply by default)
- One big idea, readable in half a second; 1–4 words of text max.
- Creator cut out on one third with a strong, clear expression + eye contact.
- High contrast between subject and background.
- A single orange/red curved arrow pointing at the most important thing — this doubles as
  Fluxomate's brand accent. Keep using it.
- A concrete number or a curiosity gap ("???", "almost didn't", "too good").
